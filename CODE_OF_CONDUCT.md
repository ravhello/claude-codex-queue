# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Expected behavior

- Show empathy and respect for differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Give and accept constructive technical feedback.
- Focus on what is best for users and the project.
- Respect privacy. Never pressure someone to share private prompts, logs,
  credentials, account details, or transcripts.
- Accept responsibility, apologize when appropriate, and learn from mistakes.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, discriminatory language, or sexualized conduct.
- Trolling, sustained disruption, or publishing another person's private data.
- Sharing credentials, private AI transcripts, or identifying diagnostics
  without the owner's explicit permission.
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, pull
requests, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct. Repeated or
serious violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban.

Report conduct incidents privately through
[GitHub private vulnerability reporting](https://github.com/ravhello/claude-codex-queue/security/advisories/new).
Maintainers will respect the reporter's privacy and respond proportionately.
